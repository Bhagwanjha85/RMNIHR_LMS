Test Methods & Interpretations

ELISA (Quantitative)

The reporting system auto-interprets results:

  • Negative: Value less than 9.0
  • Equivocal: Value between 9.0 and 11.0
  • Positive: Value greater than 11.0

RT-PCR & RAPID (Qualitative)

Manual interpretation is supported based on visual/system analysis. Recognized inputs:

  • Positive / Negative
  • Equivocal / Invalid
