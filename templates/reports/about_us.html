The Lab Management & Reporting System (LMS)

What is this Software?

This software is a custom-built Laboratory Management and Daily Reporting System designed specifically for the Viral Research & Diagnostic Laboratories (VRDL) at the Division of Virology, ICMR-RMNIHR. It provides a secure, efficient, and centralized platform to log, manage, edit, analyze, and generate professional diagnostic reports for more than 30 human pathogenic viruses of public health significance.

Why we made this Software?

To overcome the manual inefficiencies of administrative tasks and hand-signed physical workflows during large-scale patient screenings. By establishing standard test protocols, integrating a dynamic test builder, facilitating secure OTP-based logins/password resets, enabling rapid Excel bulk uploads, and auto-calculating real-time epidemiological metrics, this LMS helps track viral patterns rapidly and accurately while serving vital public health decisions.

Project Duration: 1 Month (Fast-tracked development for rapid deployment under high-security guidelines).

Software Project Workflow

01

Secure Auth & OTP

Admins and Super Admins login securely. Features multi-layered verification, Brevo API-powered email OTP codes, password recovery, and automatic session logouts on inactivity.

02

Admin Configurations

Super Admins configure test parameters, clinical methods, add authorized technicians, upload official department logos, and set header layouts dynamically.

03

Patient Logging

Technicians log patient details (Lab ID, Age, Sex, Referring Hospital, and Sample Type) alongside multi-test diagnostics using a dynamic interactive form builder.

04

Report Customization

Preview and export reports in Color or B&W. Configure signature options dynamically (Prepared By, Technician, Scientist, and I/C) for a rigorous approval process.

05

Bulk Excel Upload

Process bulk patient files via structured Excel sheets. Automate parsing and batch inserts directly to sqlite database to minimize manual transcription.

06

Real-time Analytics

Dashboard displays total sample statistics, positivity ratios, daily registration charts, sample breakdown, and administrator actions.

Division of Virology, ICMR-RMNIHR

The laboratory was established on 3rd December 2012. The Division of Virology at ICMR-Rajendra Memorial Research Institute of Medical Sciences (RMRIMS, now part of NIHR) performs basic and applied research in virology and associated pathogens. Our research focuses on molecular virology of Respiratory viruses, Flaviviruses, Gastroenteritis viruses, Oncogenic Viruses, and more. Because understanding transmission matters, we track viral variation alongside human immune responses during infections to support critical public health decisions.

As a state-of-the-art facility operating round-the-clock, the lab profiles and tracks more than 30 human pathogenic viruses. During the emergence of SARS-CoV-2 in 2020, staff swiftly launched the first PCR-based screening in Bihar, trained regional laboratories, and validated rapid diagnostic test kits. Designated as a key NACO confirmation and viral load detection hub, it serves as a central pillar for regional pandemic and disease monitoring.
